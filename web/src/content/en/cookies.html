Set by third parties

Google AdSense

When ads are visible, Google AdSense and its partners set cookies in your browser to:

  • Serve ads (required)
  • Limit ad-frequency capping
  • Personalise ads (if you consented through the CMP)
  • Measure ad performance

For the full and current list, see Google Business Safety Center.

Google Funding Choices (CMP)

For EEA / UK / Swiss visitors, the CMP writes a preference cookie storing your consent state.

Cloudflare

As CDN, Cloudflare may set __cf_bm and similar cookies for bot mitigation. See Cloudflare's cookie policy.

How to manage

  • Personalised-ad consent: click "Cookie settings" in the footer.
  • Clear all cookies: use your browser's built-in clear-data tools.
  • Disable JavaScript: the converter stops working, but all third-party tracking is also blocked.
