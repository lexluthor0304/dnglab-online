Short version

NeoAnalogLab does not collect your camera RAW files because the conversion runs entirely in your browser. The site shows ads via Google AdSense, which sets cookies under its own policy. You can withdraw consent at any time through the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

What we don't collect

  • Your images: conversion happens client-side in WebAssembly; bytes are never sent to our servers.
  • Conversion history / file names: there is no backend to log them.
  • Accounts / emails: there is no login system.

What we process

When you visit, Cloudflare (our CDN and Workers runtime) records request IP, User-Agent, and timestamp under its standard practice for DDoS protection and observability. It is not shared with third parties; retention follows Cloudflare's own policy.

Third parties: Google AdSense and Funding Choices

The site loads Google AdSense for advertising. AdSense and its partners may set cookies and read device identifiers in your browser to deliver and measure ads, including personalised ones. See Google's partner-sites policy for details.

For visitors from the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, we use Google Funding Choices as our consent management platform (CMP). You can choose "Consent to personalised ads", "Reject personalised ads", or "Pay to remove ads" (if available). Decisions are revocable any time via the footer "Cookie settings" link.

Local storage

We store these preferences in your browser's localStorage (never uploaded):

  • crt: whether the CRT scanline overlay is on
  • Language preference (read from navigator.language; not actively written)

Children

The site is not directed at or marketed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from them.

Contact
